Eggplant Parmesan

Description

Eggplant Parmesan is a baked italian dish made with crispy breaded eggplant layed with marinara, herbs, and melted mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Parmigianno Reggiano
  • Marinara
  • Mozzarella
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Dried Herbs
  • Fresh Basil and Oregano

Steps

  1. Salt and dry the eggplant
  2. Pat the eggplant dry
  3. Dip in flour
  4. Dip in egg-mixture
  5. Coat in breadcrumbs
  6. Preheat oven to 350F and bake for 25-30 min
  7. Spread marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish
  8. Add browned eggplant slices
  9. Layer with mozzarella then sauce
  10. Top with parmesan cheese then finally bake uncovered at 400F
  11. Finally garnish with fresh basil and oregano before serving

Enjoy!
