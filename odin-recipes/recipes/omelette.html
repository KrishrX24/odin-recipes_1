Omelette

Description

Delicious eggs wisked together in a pan and served like a folded pancake.

Ingredients

  • 3 large fresh eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon cold water
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 pinch of cayenne or black pepper to taste (Optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk together the eggs, water, and salt in a bowl until fully blended in
  2. Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter in a nonstick pan over medium heat
  3. Once the butter is melted, pour in the eggs and stir, shaking the pan and scrambling the eggs
  4. Add toppings such as arugala, baby spinach, cherry tomatoes, and/or cheese
  5. Reduce the heat once solidified and fold over or something
  6. Slide the omelette carefully out of the pan and serve with buttery toast and some fruit

Enjoy!
